The Risks of Lying on Your CV

jobseeker.com

When putting together your CV, it's important to present yourself in the best light while maintaining honesty. It can be tempting to embellish qualifications, but staying truthful is crucial. Your CV serves as your initial introduction to employers, showcasing your strengths while accurately reflecting your skills and experience. In today's digital age, with rigorous screening processes in place, any discrepancies can be quickly uncovered, potentially damaging your credibility. Despite this, some candidates still choose to take liberties with the truth.
